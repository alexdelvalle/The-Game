Caffeine Canteen

Can you stay awake?

How To Play:

Welcome to Caffeine Canteen!

The goal of the game is for you to stay awake through all the hustle and bustle throughout your day!

How to Win: Throw your mug into the air and consume all the available coffee to keep your energy level up.

Be careful, though! Your energy decreases as time passes, but you can increase it by consuming at least 10 cups of coffee. Make sure you don't fall asleep early!

Controls:

  • Use the left and right arrows to move left and right.
  • Use the spacebar to throw your mug and capture the falling coffee.

Happy Caffeinating!
