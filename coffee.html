Caffeine Canteen

Can you survive?

How To Play:

Welcome to Caffeine Canteen!

The goal of the game is for you to stay awake through all the hustle and bustle throughout your day! Grab as much caffeine as you can by throwing your mug into the air and capturing the coffee before the day ends.

Be careful, though! Don't capture any items that will decrease your caffeine level. You wouldn't want to fall asleep early!

Controls:

  • Use the left and right arrows to move left and right.
  • Use the spacebar to throw your mug and capture the falling coffee.

Happy Caffeinating!
